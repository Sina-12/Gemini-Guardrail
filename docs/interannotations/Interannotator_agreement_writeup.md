For our interannotator agreement study, we selected Krippendorff’s Alpha as our primary metric. Unlike Cohen’s or Fleiss’ Kappa, Alpha is capable of handling our entire four-person team (Sina, Jai, Gilly, and Rayan) simultaneously. Crucially, we utilized an ordinal level of measurement because our annotation schema (0, 1, 2) is rank-ordered; this ensures that a disagreement between a 0 (Failure) and a 2 (Perfect) is weighted more heavily than a minor disagreement between a 1 and a 2. While initial testing on a micro-sample yielded zero scores due to a lack of statistical variance, we expect our final 50-item gold standard to provide a reliable measure of our team's alignment on argument success and accuracy.
